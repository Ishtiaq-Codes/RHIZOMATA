Rules of the Realm

How to Play

1. Choose Your Identity

Begin by drawing an Identity Card. You are bound to an element — Earth, Water, Fire, or Air. This is your anchor, determining your strengths and vulnerabilities in the game.

2. Read the Situation

Draw a Situation Card to reveal the moral and tactical challenges facing your realm. Your response must align with your true nature.

3. Move Your Pawns

Control two pawns simultaneously: Stability and Ambition. Navigate the interconnected board. Every move ripples outwards, shifting the balance of the board for everyone.

Winning the Game

Victory is not simply reaching the center first. True mastery requires perfectly balancing your Ambition and Stability pawns while honoring your elemental Identity. The player whose choices best reflect their true nature claims the realm.
